SINDHUJA MADABUSHI

I am a PhD student in the Computer Science department at Virginia Tech advised by Jin-Hee Cho. My current research is focusses on privacy, security, fairness, and incentives in federated learning, an approach that enables collaborative machine learning while safeguarding individual data privacy.

Previously, I conducted research at University of Wisconsin-Madison with Parmeswaran Ramanathan where I developed privacy-preserving algorithms for human genome read alignment.
